NovaNode

We are a subsidiary company of Spartan Robotics Incorporated competing in the BEST Robotics competition.

BEST (Boosting Engineering Science and Technology) Robotics is a 8-week-long competition getting students interested in engineering careers and building skills sought after in the workforce through problem-solving and experience working with industry professionals and technology. The competition consists of 5 portions evaluated: exhibit, notebook, marketing, spirit, and robot. Through the competition, BEST Robotics is fostering the next generation of young professionals by equipping them with the skills that the industry seeks in its workforce.

The Game: "Factoids"

The 2025 B.E.S.T. Robotics game, called "FACTOIDS", is themed around artificial intelligence and data training. Teams design robots that collect “Factoids,” which represent pieces of information scattered around the field. Some factoids are useful (“true”) and others are misleading (“false”), and teams must gather, sort, and process them carefully to score points. Robots also place factoids into “neural network” structures to simulate training an AI system, build connections within the network for bonuses, and activate field mechanisms to access more data. The game this year teaches students about engineering, strategy, and the importance of data quality, mirroring how real AI systems learn from the information they’re given.
